Exercise

Cardiovascular Health

  • Exercise 30 minutes daily (walking, jogging, cycling)
  • Maintain healthy weight (BMI 18.5-24.9)
  • Limit salt intake to less than 5g per day
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol
  • Regular BP and cholesterol checkups

Mental Wellness

  • Practice meditation or mindfulness daily
  • Get 7-8 hours of quality sleep
  • Stay socially connected with friends/family
  • Take breaks from screens and social media
  • Seek professional help when needed

Balanced Diet

  • Eat 5 servings of fruits and vegetables daily
  • Choose whole grains over refined grains
  • Include lean proteins (fish, chicken, legumes)
  • Drink 8-10 glasses of water daily
  • Limit processed foods and added sugars

Physical Activity

  • 150 minutes moderate exercise per week
  • Include strength training 2x per week
  • Take stairs instead of elevators
  • Stand and stretch every hour if sitting
  • Find activities you enjoy for consistency

Diabetes Prevention

  • Maintain healthy body weight
  • Regular blood sugar monitoring if at risk
  • Reduce refined carbohydrates intake
  • Increase fiber-rich foods
  • Annual HbA1c testing after age 45

Cancer Prevention

  • Avoid tobacco in all forms
  • Limit alcohol consumption
  • Protect skin from excessive sun exposure
  • Get vaccinated (HPV, Hepatitis B)
  • Regular cancer screenings as recommended

Bone Health

  • Adequate calcium intake (1000-1200mg daily)
  • Vitamin D supplementation if needed
  • Weight-bearing exercises regularly
  • Avoid excessive caffeine and alcohol
  • Bone density scan after age 65

Eye Care

  • Follow 20-20-20 rule for screen time
  • Wear sunglasses with UV protection
  • Eat foods rich in omega-3 and vitamins
  • Regular eye exams every 1-2 years
  • Maintain proper lighting while reading

Emergency Warning Signs

Seek immediate medical attention if you experience:

• Chest pain or pressure
• Difficulty breathing
• Sudden severe headache
• Weakness or numbness
• Severe abdominal pain
• Uncontrolled bleeding
